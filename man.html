Here are some delicious recipies to enjoy an amazing meal at home.

  • Pancakes
  • Ramen
PANCAKES
  1. Prepare batter for pancakes with pancake mix and water in adequate amount.
  2. Now allow the batter to be medium thick.
  3. Place a pan and wait till its hot,now use batter and make pancakes by spreading batter over the pan (YOU CAN GET CREATIVE!).
  4. Take the pancakes out of pan and enjoy these hoties witha dash of maple syrup at the top.
RAMEN
  • Use a packet of market ramen noodles.
  • Open the packet and place ingredients into the boiling water.
  • Add adequate amount of salt in water.
  • Leave it for about 5 min and make sure that you have added all spices according to your choice.
  • Now take out the cooked ramen in a bowl and enjoy your meal with a dash of chilli sauce.
